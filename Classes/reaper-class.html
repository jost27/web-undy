UNIVERSE CLASSES MYTHS MULTIMEDIA

A Reaper is a MYTH that will force their targets to keep their guard up as they roam through all jungle. Reapers have the innate ability to climb terrain and perform parkour so they can gain the crucial element of surprise and assassinate their foes in style. Burst damage and mobility are the Reaper's bywords, their enemies will flee in terror or face elimination from the world.
