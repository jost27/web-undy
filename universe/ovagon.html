UNIVERSE CLASSES MYTHS MULTIMEDIA
Under the influence of Tempia, Ovagon is a timeless futuristic realm comprised of seven Districts, where the sun never sets, and days never elapse. It is ruled under the strict bureaucracy of the Nine Archons; mysterious beings capable of seeing the possible futures of all inhabitants. In Ovagon, everything works with precise clock-like synchrony. Using the shadows from the floating districts, the nine could control all living hours below. To keep order, the Capitol instructed the military district to expand its forces through all lower districts. When tension between the many oppressed masses and the unyielding rulers became uncontainable, an opportunistic military coup emerged seeking all power and sending the entire realm into total disarray.
