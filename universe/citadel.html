Under the influence of Marah, Algor became a deserted realm, afflicted by snow and a constant frigid climate. Life became so scarce in Algor that the omnipresent Essentia Particles began infusing life into the cold terrain. As a result, Algor hosts a living mountain range which divides it into multiple complex landscapes, each giving rise to unique ways for Life to blossom.
